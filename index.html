Arm Hypertrophy App

Minimalist, science-based, single-file, and mildly obsessed with getting your biceps and triceps bigger using exactly your equipment: kettlebells 35/50/70 and dumbbells 25/35.
Frequency: 2–3x/week
Reps: 6–20+
Effort: 0–3 RIR
ROM: full or nearly full

Principles used
  • Broad rep ranges can build muscle when sets are close enough to failure.
  • Around 10+ weekly hard sets per muscle is a strong general target.
  • Full ROM and loaded lengthened positions are often useful.
  • Overhead triceps work biases the long head.
  • Supinated, neutral, and pronated curls cover the elbow flexors more completely.
Progression ladder
  1. Add reps
  2. Improve ROM and control
  3. Slow the eccentric to 3–5 sec
  4. Add a stretch pause
  5. Add a set
  6. Use myo-reps or rest-pause
  7. Move to a heavier implement if reps allow
Warm-up
  • 2–3 min general movement
  • Arm circles and easy elbow/wrist prep
  • 1–2 ramp-up sets for first curl and first triceps move
Rest times
  • Heavy movements: 2–3 min
  • Isolation work: 60–90 sec
  • Rest-pause mini-rests: 20–30 sec
If your weights feel too light, use unilateral work, longer eccentrics, pauses in the stretch, and rest-pause sets. Fancy suffering, scientifically arranged.
